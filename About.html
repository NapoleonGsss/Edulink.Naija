About EduLinkNaija

Created by Oluyede Samuel and Adewuyi Taiwo
for one purpose — to make education accessible to every child, everywhere.

Our Story

EduLinkNaija began as a simple idea — that knowledge should never be locked behind distance, privilege, or expensive resources. In many rural communities, bright young minds lose their spark not because of lack of intelligence, but because of lack of opportunity. We wanted to change that.

Founded by two students, Oluyede Samuel and Adewuyi Taiwo, the mission was clear: build a free, digital bridge between underserved students and the world of learning. We envisioned a platform where any child, even in the most remote village, could open a phone or computer and find books, past questions, and interactive quizzes — tools that help them grow, compete, and dream beyond borders.

What began as a small community project has grown into a movement to democratize education through technology. Each page, each resource, and each quiz is a promise — that no learner should be left behind.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide free, accessible learning materials and digital tools for students in underprivileged and rural communities.

Vision

To build a generation of self-taught innovators, critical thinkers, and dreamers empowered through technology and knowledge.
